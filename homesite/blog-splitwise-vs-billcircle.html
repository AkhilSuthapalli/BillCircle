Bill Circle vs. Splitwise

Splitwise is a mature tool, but maturity often brings complexity. Bill Circle was built for users who want to split expenses fast, privately, and without the friction of mandatory accounts.

Link-First Access

Skip the onboarding. Create a circle and share a secure, non-guessable link instantly.

Privacy by Design

No public profiles, no contact list imports, and no searchable users. Your data stays yours.

Zero Friction

Anyone with the link can participate. No sign-ups, no passwords, and no app downloads.

Lowering Cognitive Load

Splitwise focuses on long-term tracking and friend networks. Bill Circle focuses on the core tasks: Add members, log expenses, see who owes whom, and lock the record when done.

Minimal Friction

One-Time Settlement

Optimized for Temporary Groups

Trips, dinners, and hackathons are temporary. Once settled, you can lock your circle to freeze the results and preserve the final balance forever.
